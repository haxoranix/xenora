ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Dr. Aris Thorne is an exo-anthropologist and award-winning author whose work explores the intersection of alien civilizations and human consciousness.

With a background in astrophysics and cultural anthropology, Dr. Thorne brings a unique perspective to science fiction, creating worlds that feel both scientifically plausible and culturally rich.

When not writing, Dr. Thorne serves as a consultant to space agencies and participates in SETI research initiatives.
